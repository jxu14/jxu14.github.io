STEAK

Steak is one of my favorite foods. I liked it cook medium rare, since it maintains most of the juice and it's soft. Outback steakhouse makes great steak, and the seasoning is very important.

DONUTS

Donuts are my favorite sugary food. I like them in all flavors, chocolate, glazed, sugar, you name it. You can eat them at any time during the day and as dessert. They're just amazing.

Baby Back Ribs

Baby back ribs are super messy but super good. Armadillo Willys makes great ribs since they slow cook them for 8 hours. Every time I eat them, I eat more than a full slab and I get very messy hands and face.
